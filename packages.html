A "text of travel packages" refers to the descriptive content that a travel agency uses to promote and sell travel packages, which often include a catchy title, tour overview, itinerary highlights, inclusions/exclusions, activity details, and images to entice customers to book a vacation. These texts are found in promotional materials like SMS messages, website descriptions, brochures, and social media posts, outlining details such as destination, duration, accommodation, activities, and pricing to create an appealing offer for a combined set of travel services.
